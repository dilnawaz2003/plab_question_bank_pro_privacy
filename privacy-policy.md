# Privacy Policy for PLAB Question Bank Pro

**Effective Date:** May 11, 2026

Welcome to **PLAB Question Bank Pro**. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information you may share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

---

### 1. Information Collection and Use
**PLAB Question Bank Pro** is designed to be an offline-first application. 
- **Personal Information:** We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information (such as your name, email address, phone number, or location).
- **Usage Data:** We do not track your usage behavior or collect analytics data through any third-party services.
- **User-Generated Content:** Any data you create within the app—such as bookmarks, quiz results, notes, and OSCE scenarios—is stored strictly on your device's local storage.

### 2. Permissions and Data Access
To provide specific features, the app may request the following permissions:
- **Camera and Photo Library:** Used only if you choose to add images to your OSCE scenario notes. These images are processed locally and are not uploaded to any server.
- **Notifications:** Used to provide study reminders and alerts. These are managed locally on your device.
- **Storage:** Used to save your progress, database, and settings on your device.

### 3. Third-Party Services
We do not use any third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising services (such as Firebase Analytics, AdMob, or Facebook SDK). Your data remains entirely within the app environment on your device.

### 4. Data Security
Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on the security of your mobile device. We recommend using standard device security features (like PIN or biometric lock) to protect your information.

### 5. Children’s Privacy
Our application does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

### 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app or on the app store page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
